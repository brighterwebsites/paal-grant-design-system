Paal GrantPrincipal Designer

Hi, I'm Paal. I'm a landscape designer creating calm, grounded gardens shaped by climate, place, and the way people want to live.

At Paal Grant Designs in Landscaping, we design and build bespoke residential landscapes across Victoria and beyond, offering both landscape design packages and full construction delivery.

My work has been recognised internationally, including Best Design Award at the Gardening World Cup in Japan and Gold Medals at the Melbourne International Flower & Garden Show.

Each garden is individually designed — combining architectural clarity with natural, climate-responsive planting to create spaces that feel effortless, enduring, and deeply connected to their environment.

How We Work

Our Process

01
Consultation

Every project begins with a conversation. We meet onsite or virtually to understand your space, how you live and what you want to achieve. A paid onsite consultation is $350, fully credited toward any design package.

Onsite or Virtual  ·  $350 credited
02
Design

All designs are developed in full 3D visualisation. You will see and understand your garden completely before a single decision is made about construction.

"Our designs are presented in 3D so you can see and understand your garden before it is built — including levels and structure, materials and layout, planting zones and the overall spatial experience."

Levels & Structure  ·  Materials & Layout  ·  Planting Zones
03
Construction Quote

Once the design is approved, a detailed construction quote is prepared. All pricing is transparent and scope-based. Clients who wish to use their own builder can take the documentation to any qualified contractor.

Transparent Pricing  ·  No Hidden Costs
04
Construction

Our experienced construction team manages the entire build. From groundworks and hard landscaping through to planting and final handover — every element executed with care and precision. Design and build capability in one studio.

Full Project Delivery  ·  Expert Build Team

The Studio

About Paal Grant

Paal GrantPrincipal Designer & Founder

Paal Grant Designs In Landscaping is a design-led practice with over 20 years of experience creating site-responsive residential landscapes across Victoria and beyond.

We are not a generic landscaping company. Our work begins with a genuine understanding of how people want to live — and then we design and build landscapes that reflect that vision with rigour, craft and care.

Every project is individually designed. We do not use templates. The result is a landscape that belongs to its site, its climate, and the people who live there.

Our practice encompasses both landscape design and full construction delivery. Clients can engage us at any stage — design only, or complete end-to-end project delivery.
