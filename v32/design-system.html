CSS Logo Filter Technique

Dark Background (White Logo)

filter: brightness(0) invert(1);
opacity: 0.85–0.9;

Works on any PNG/SVG logo — forces to white regardless of original colour

Light Background (Dark Logo)

filter: brightness(0);
opacity: 0.75–0.88;

Forces to black on parchment bg. Reduce opacity to match tone.

Hover State

transition: opacity 0.28s;
:hover { opacity: 1; }

Subtle full-opacity reveal on hover. No colour change needed.
